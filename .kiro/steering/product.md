# Product Context: PostMortem.dev

PostMortem.dev is a utility for engineers to automate the most tedious part of incident management: documentation. 

**Target Audience:** SREs, DevOps Engineers, and students.
**Core Value:** Speed and structure. It takes raw, unstructured data and applies industry-standard postmortem formatting.
**Key Differentiator:** Direct browser-to-LLM integration for privacy and speed.
